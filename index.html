Cosmic Dream Art

“A visionary who captures the imagination”

Ronald Michael Jenkins is a Conceptualist, an Abstract Impressionistic Artist who invites your curiosity into the realm of imagination via dimensional travel while becoming One with “The OverMind” of “The Creator.”

RMJ experiences the wonders of “Creation” through various forms of energy, constantly evolving and reconfiguring itself by vibrational transmissions, dreams and intuitive feelings.

He literally becomes a Channeler for “The Creator” to communicate information and messages through “Cosmic Dream Art.”

From the tiniest particles of matter in the recesses of inner space, to the largest black holes in outer space, RMJ is continually inspired by the “Oneness” and magnificence of “All of What Is, Creation.” His assignment is to express the thoughts of “The OverSoul.”
